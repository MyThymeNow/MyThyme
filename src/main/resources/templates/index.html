MyThyme

MyThyme is a digital cookbook, designed to let users create grocery lists and recipes. Users can also save and share their created grocery lists, while also being provided recommended recipes based on search bar entries. MyThyme was created by a group of 4 developers, utilizing Java, JavaScript, MySQL, Spring Boot, HTML, CSS and several APIs.
